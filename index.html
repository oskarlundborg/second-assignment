HQ

Legion Praetor 210 pts

The praetors were the mightiest warriors and battle-leaders of the ancient Legiones Astartes, second only to the demigod-like primarchs in martial skills and generalship. They most commonly commanded Chapter-size formations within their Legion or whatever the equivalently-named unit was within a given Legion's culture.
